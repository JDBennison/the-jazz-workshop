Welcome to The Jazz Workshop

The Jazz Workshop is a jazz education programme based in Sussex. Our goal is to pair a positive, group learning environment with a methodical and accessible approach to learning jazz music.

Arabella is a diligent and motivated teacher. Her deep love of jazz, and her exhaustive knowledge of its forms, is evident in her inspiring workshops.
